About Us

You will find our club directly facing Cullercoats beach and harbour. It was established in 1945 and affiliated with the Club & Institute Union in 1947. Cullercoats, in the 1800s and 1900s, was a well-known and vibrant centre for small-boat fishing, with many links to local history and folklore. It has also been home to an artist's colony. One of the colony's most famous artists, Winslow Homer, gave his name to the adjacent block of luxury flats, Winslow Court. Another interesting fact is that if you visit Google Earth and search for Cullercoats, only one hit is registered, as our Cullercoats is the only Cullercoats in the world.

Look Inside

Concert Room

Our epic concert room is perfect for live music, functions and events. We have a seating capacity of 200 guests and a standing capacity of 400 guests.

Buffet Bar

Our more modest buffet bar is just the setting for a casual evening, small event or live music. We have a seating capacity 90 guests. Our buffet bar comes with a free jukebox.

Sports Bar

At our sports bar, we have all major sports events running on the multiple TV screens spotted around the room. You can also enjoy a game of darts, pool, or snooker. We have a seating capacity of 120 guests.

Seaview Lounge

If you are looking for a more relaxed and cozy setting, our seaview lounge offers just that. A smaller space with a wonderful view of Cullercoats Bay and the Mouth of the Tyne.
